Text Cleanup

The text cleanup is a tool to help you generating a 'cleaner' text without too many empty spaces.

  • It eliminates excessive multiple spaces between, before and after words: 'hi      there' becomes 'hi there'
  • It eliminates spaces before dots, question marks and exclamations: 'hi there  !' becomes 'hi there!'
  • It adds spaces after dots, question marks and exclamations where there are none: 'hi.hello' becomes 'hi. hello'
  • It does not remove line breaks, such as in:
    'hey

     ho'
